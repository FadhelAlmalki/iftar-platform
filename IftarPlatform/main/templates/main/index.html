FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about using Iftar Platform.

The Iftar Platform is a national digital system that organizes and regulates Iftar initiatives. It connects Owners, Organizers, and Government authorities to ensure all Iftar activities are properly managed, approved, and compliant with regulations.

The platform is designed for three types of users: Owners who create and manage Iftar initiatives, Organizers who apply to host Iftar events and Government (Admin) who review and approve requests.

You need to register as an Owner, log in to your account, and submit a new initiative by providing details such as location, city, and description. The initiative will then be reviewed by the Government authority.

After signing up as an Organizer, you can browse available initiatives and submit a permit request for the one you want. Your request will be reviewed by the Government authority before approval.

All submitted initiatives and permit requests go through a review process. The Government authority evaluates them and either approves or rejects them based on compliance with rules and regulations.

You can track the status of permit requests through your my permits page. Once approved, you will receive an official permit, which may include a downloadable PDF with a QR code for verification.

Yes, all approved Iftar permits are valid for a specific time period. Organizers must ensure that their activities take place within the approved timeframe.
